About the Founder — Fatine Suleiman

I created Beyaz from my passion for quality, beauty and meaningful design. I believe shoes are more than fashion — they carry us through life's most important moments.

My vision is to blend cultures, craftsmanship and character into every pair. By working with skilled artisans in Istanbul and sourcing fine Turkish leathers, I ensure that every shoe tells a story of tradition, strength and sophistication.

Beyaz reflects who I am — bold yet elegant, passionate yet refined.
