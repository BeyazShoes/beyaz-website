Wholesale & How It Works

Partnering with boutiques, retailers, and stylists who appreciate exceptional footwear.

We partner with boutiques, retailers, stylists and design houses who appreciate fine footwear. Our goal is to be a dependable supplier and collaborator for your business.

Who We Work With

Independent boutiques and concept stores — fashion retailers seeking private-label partnerships — stylists and costume designers requiring bespoke footwear — international distributors looking for unique, handcrafted shoes.

How Ordering Works

  • Browse our catalog and select the styles you're interested in.
  • Submit an inquiry with your desired quantities, sizes and any customization requests.
  • We'll confirm pricing, lead time and minimum order quantities (MOQs).
  • Once you approve, we begin production and keep you updated throughout the process.
  • Your order ships securely to your location. We offer worldwide shipping on wholesale orders.

Shipping & Lead Times

We ship to North America, Europe, the Middle East and Asia. Standard production lead times range from 4–9 weeks depending on the style and volume. Expedited production may be available upon request.

Customization Options

If you have a particular design in mind, we offer custom colorways, materials and hardware finishes for select styles. Please contact us to discuss feasibility and minimums.
